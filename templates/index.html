Municipal Corporation Management refers to the administration and governance of a municipal corporation, which is a local government entity responsible for providing essential public services and infrastructure to residents within its jurisdiction. .

Municipal corporations are typically established in urban areas and cities to manage and oversee various aspects of local governance, including urban planning, public health, sanitation, water supply, waste management, transportation, and other municipal services.
